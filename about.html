About Us

Mike Geryak Construction is dedicated to providing customers with a positive building experience and a quality custom-built home from start to finish. As a builder in Hancock County and the southern parts of Hamilton County areas since 1990, our experience, practical knowledge of the industry, and professional team are available to assist you in your new construction project and site development. Contact us to schedule a consultation and learn how Mike Geryak Construction Custom Home Builder operates. We will provide you with excellent, personalized service and customer satisfaction.
